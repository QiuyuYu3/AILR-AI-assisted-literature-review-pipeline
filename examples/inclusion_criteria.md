## Inclusion
- **I1 — Empirical fNIRS study**: reports original collection and/or analysis of (f)NIRS data.
- **I2 — Full paper available**: published as a journal article, conference paper, or online preprint.

## Exclusion
- **E1 — Review or meta-analysis** (no original data).
- **E2 — Abstract only**: conference proceedings without a complete paper.
- **E3 — Dissertation or thesis.**
